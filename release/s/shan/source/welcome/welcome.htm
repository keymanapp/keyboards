Mobile

1. The Shan keyboard layout for Mobile devices (tables/phones), note it is slightly different than the desktop version due to layout

2. The Shan keyboard layout for Mobile devices (tables/phones), with Shift key pressed

Note: On mobile platforms, the tone marks are on the shift layer, additionally, for those who prefer it, they are also on the g-l keys through long-pressing/popup the keys, i.e. long press g give you the option for း Long press h give you the option for ႇ etc.
Long press on the period key will give you access to a number of punctations.
Long press on the number keys give you access to Burmese/Shan numbers.
When long-pressing/popup, you need to slide finger to the desired character then release.
