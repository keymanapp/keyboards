Start Using Hâts (east)

Roman keyboard for typing vowel length (circumflex "hats" over vowels) in roman orthography (East Cree).

Hats over long vowels (and tilde over "n") are available by typing two letters in a row. Thus, typing "aa" would become "â", and typing "nn" would become "ñ". These are available for aiun and AIUN.
