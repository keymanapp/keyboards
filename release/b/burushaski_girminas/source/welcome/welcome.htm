Burushaski Orthography and Keyboard      

Overview

Burushaski orthography encompasses the spelling and punctuations of the Burushaski language. This particular orthography has been proposed and agreed upon by representatives of all three varieties of Burushaski of Hunza, Yasin and Nagar. The unique characters of Burushaski, mostly retroflexes and diacritic vowels have been colored in this document.

Burushaski Girminas is latin/Roman based keyboard developed for Buruśo Mahraka, and it is available for free.

Please follow the key combinations for the extra Buruśaski characters. Most of the characters will preceed with /dot/, unless the dot has already been used in another rule. Only Ć and Ś have different rule. Ś will be the output of S + H and Ć will be the output of C + C.

In order to write just /sh/, you have to press s +h+h.

Using the keyboard on touch devices

On mobile devices, where keyman engine and Burushaski Girminas is installed, press and hold the main character for the accentuating characters to appear. A seperate android keyboard is available in the google play store for download.

 Please download here: Burushaski Girminas

Since the keyboard is a beta version, feedback and recommendations are welcomed.
