Physical Keyboard layout

Main features:

  1. Based on QWERTY that we are all familiar with (US basic layout), but without the X and Q letters.
  2. Put 'pangkon' and 'pepet' (schwa) in place of X and Q
  3. Put 'adeg-adeg' to the right of 'L' and 'pada lungsi' and 'pada lingsa' in the places of ',' and '.'.

Touch Keyboard layout

Touch keyboard features:

  1. Based on the physical keyboard layout
  2. Basic: 4 layers (default, shift, numeric, symbol), 4 rows, 10 keys wide. It means large buttons, ease to type keys.
  3. All the necessary symbols (and nothing else) on the default layer (adeg-adeg, pada lungsi, pada lingsa) punctuations. The rest of the Javanese script symbols are in the symbol layer.

Tips:

  1. Just type naturally!
