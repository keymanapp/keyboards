Start Using wenchow開始使用溫州話羅馬字輸入法

wenchow for üe-tsiu-‘ó.溫州話羅馬字

typing specific letters by doubletapping and combination 雙擊和字母組合輸入特殊字母

examples: oo > ö, uu > û, ii > ï, ae > æ, uw > ü, mm > m̌, nn > ň, ll > l̆, ňg > ngg

adding aspiration marks "‘" by adding h after consonants 在輔音後輸入h字母，添加送氣音符號"‘"

examples: ph > p‘, th > t‘, kh > k‘, tsh > ts‘, chh > ch‘, kwh > kw‘, hh > ‘

doubletapping h, to generating voiced sound h "‘", 雙擊h，產生濁音h "‘"

examples: hh > ‘

typing tone marks by adding "q" and "x" 通過添加字母q和x輸入音調符號

examples: aq > á, ax > à.

cancelling letter tranformation and tone marks by typing the tranformation key again

再次點擊轉化鍵，取消字母轉化和音調符號

examples: aqq > aq, axx > ax, ooo > oo.
