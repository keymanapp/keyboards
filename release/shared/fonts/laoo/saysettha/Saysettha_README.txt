Saysettha OT
Saysettha MX
========================

Thank you for your interest in the Saysettha OT and Saysettha MX fonts.
We hope you find them useful!

These fonts provide glyphs for a wide range of Lao characters.

Saysettha MX is optimised for macOS and Linux, and Saysettha OT works
well on other systems.

Both fonts are released under the SIL Open Font License.

See the OFL and OFL-FAQ for details of the SIL Open Font License.
