hakka keyboard for hakka language

Hakka for Hak-kâ-ngî (Hakka language) Pha̍k-và-sṳ̀.

Hakka keyboard, is using "sfrj" keys for generating tone marks

rules for representing tones:

2=s, 3=f, 5=r, 7=j

examples:

as > á, af > à, ar > â, aj > a̍

shortcuts:

aa > â, ee > ê, x > chh, z > tsh

specific letters:

uu > ṳ

doubletaping leads to generate original letters:

例example: fijj > fij, ass > as, barr > bar, luuu > luu
