For phone

While most of the layout remains the same as in desktop version, numerals have been moved to separate layer and some characters have been moved to longpress menu or flick to account for limited screen estate. Wherever a long press menu is available, there is a small dot on right top of the key.

Notable longpresses

  • ीइई are in ि
  • ैएऐ are in े
  • ूउऊ are in ु
  • डढध are in द
  • र्‍ is in र
  • ष is in स
  • क्ष is in छ
  • ज्ञ is in ग

Special Characters

ZWJ and ZWNJ are in the shift layer.

For tablet

Similar to desktop layout for most part apart from some long press items. Symbols that required dead key combinations are in symbols layer.
