<h1>Keyboard Layout</h1>

<h2>Desktop Keyboard Layout</h2>
<p> The letters 

On a desktop keyboard, the letters 'á Á é É í Í ó Ó ú Ú' do not have their own keys, but are made by pressing ';' and then the vowel. For example, pressing ';' and then 'a' makes 'á', pressing ';' and then 'A' makes 'Á'.

<h2>Mobile Keyboard Layout</h2>
<p> On a phone or tablet, you can see and select 'á Á é É í Í ó Ó ú Ú' if you press and hold on a vowel. For example, pressing and holding 'a' will show the option to type 'á', pressing and holding 'A' will show the option to type 'Á'. </p>
