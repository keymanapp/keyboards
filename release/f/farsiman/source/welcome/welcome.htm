Farsiman keyboard

The English letter allows you to write in Farsi all you have to do is to know the sound of the letters and you've got the equivalent letters in Farsi before your eyes.

If you know the familiar English keyboard, you can write skillfully in Farsi, too. The letter ‘s’ shows ‘sin’ and ‘k’ shows ‘kaaf’, so to write 'motoshakker, you type, ‘m’+t+‘S’+‘k’+‘r’.
