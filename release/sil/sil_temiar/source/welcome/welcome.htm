Temiar Keyboard.

Desktop Usage

This follows standard US / Malay layout with the following keystrokes for vowels:

e +
"`" > "è"
"'" > "é"
"~" > "ẽ"

E +
"`" > "È"
"'" > "É"
"=" > "Ë"
"~" > "Ẽ"

i +
"`" > 'ì'
"~" > 'ĩ'

I +
"`" > "Ì"
"~" > 'Ĩ'

o +
"`" > 'ò'
"~" > 'õ'

O +
"`" > "Ò"
"~"" > 'Õ'

A +
"~" > 'Ã'
"~"" > 'ã'

u +
"~"" > 'ũ'

U +
"~" > 'Ũ'

Glottal Stop
"|" > "ꞌ"
