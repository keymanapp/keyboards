Latinized Urdu

Urdu With Latin Alphabets

Latinizes The Entire Urdu way of writing from Arabic-based letters to Latin-based letters on the basis of one letter-per-sound. It builds on the currently existing Turkish alphabets based on the sound that are already common between Urdu and Turkish languages, further more it adds some diacritical marks on the letters for aspirated sounds in Urdu and long vowel sounds and nasalised vowel sounds.
