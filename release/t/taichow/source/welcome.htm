taichow keyboard for romanized goetian T'e-ciu-'wa Ng-Yüih-Nyü

台州話寧波話蘇州話吳越語羅馬字輸入法

typing specific letters by doubletapping and combination 雙擊和字母組合輸入特殊字母

examples: oo > ô, oe > ö, uu > ü, ae > æ, aw > ă, aa > ă, uw > ŭ

adding aspiration marks "‛" by adding h after consonants 在輔音後輸入h字母，添加送氣音符號"‛"

examples: ph > p‛, th > t‛, kh > k‛, tsh > ts‛, ch > c‛, kwh > kw‛, hh > ‛, kyh > ky‛, kwh > kw‛, khy > ky‛, khw > kw‛， gh > ‛

Typing 'gh', to generating voiced sound h "‛", 輸入gh，產生濁音h "‛"

examples: gh > ‛

typing rising and falling tone marks by adding "q" and "x" 通過添加字母q和x輸入上聲和去聲音調符號

examples: aq > á, ax > à.

cancelling letter tranformation and tone marks by typing the tranformation key again

再次點擊轉化鍵，取消字母轉化和音調符號

examples: aqq > aq, axx > ax, ooo > oo.
