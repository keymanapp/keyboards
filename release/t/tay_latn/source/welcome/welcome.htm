Start Using Tày Latin

This is a keyboard that contains all of the characters needed to type the Tày language using the Latin script. The keyboard produces NFC Unicode.

  • a or A followed by w or W produces ă or Ă and o, O, u, or U followed by w or W produces the vowel with a horn.
  • Typing an "a", "e" or "o" twice produces the vowel with a circumflex. Typing an "o" a third time will convert to "oo".
  • Any vowel (aAeEiIoOuUyYâÂêÊôÔăĂăĂơƠưƯ) followed by "f", "s", "r", "j", "x" will produce the vowel plus grave, acute, hook above, dot below, or tilde above respectively.
  • Any vowel (aAeEiIoOuUyYâÂêÊôÔăĂăĂơƠưƯ) followed by "q" or "k" will produce the vowel plus macron below or double grave above respectively.
  • Typing "d" or "D" twice will produce "đ" or "Đ" respectively.
