Toto Unicode Keyboarding

This keyboard is for typing the Toto language in the Toto script. There are approximately 2000 speakers of the Toto language, living on the Northern border of West Bengal, India. Dhaniram Toto designed the characters of the Toto script. It was launched in May 2015 and in 2021 the characters of the script were accepted into the Unicode standard.

This package includes the Noto Serif Toto font from Google
