Taigi SDWXV for Tâi-gí (Taiwanese) pe̍h-ōe-jī.

This IME selected "sdwxv" for representing tone marks

rules:

  • 2=s, 3=d, 5=w, 7=x, 8=x, 9=v, ⁿ=nn, o͘=oo
    • examples:
  • as > á, ad > à, ax > ā, aw > â, ahx > a̍h
    • shortcut:
  • aa > â, ee > ê, uu > û, ii > î
    • doubletaping leads to generate original letters:
  • example: fix = fixx, did = didd, kann = kannn, law = laww, bee = beee
